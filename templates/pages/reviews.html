Food

I like to keep track of the places I eat at and I also have a systematic way of ranking food as well. I only rate the food; not the prices, atmosphere, or service, because it is the food that will get me coming back. This system is also not linear on a scale of 1-10, for example an *OK* restaurant would be around a 6/10, not 4/10. The following is how I rank food:

  • Bad

    This is food that does not taste good even if I'm hungry, it would either be bland or have some kind of unpleasant flavor. Food can range from bad to absolutely terrible, but I don't think separating those are useful.

  • Ok

    Most food lands in this category, it still tastes really good if I'm hungry. Although I would not grumble if this was suggested among friends, there are obvious improvements to be made. This doesn't mean the place is below average though, on a scale of 1-10 this is something like a 6.

  • Good

    When I think food is good, that means it's above average, but there are still subtle things that could be improved. This is the kind of place I'd go to regularly if I lived close by.

  • Great

    Great food is perfectly executed and as great as I could imagine it. This is the kind of place I'd go to often if I lived in the same city.

  • AMAZING

    This means I did not know this kind of food could be this good. It was perfectly executed and showed me a side of food beyond my imagination. If I was visiting the area, I would make sure to go here.

I'm not a professional cook or anything, just a self-proclaimed foodie. I also keep track of the date here because a restaurant's quality can change a lot over time. For the AMAZING locations, I've also included my favourite menu item in hopes you'd order it too!

Anime

This is a collection of reviews for anime I've watched. That said, this list is as biased as you could get towards my personal tastes, so don't expect objective opinions here. For rating anime, I think in a very compartmentalized way, so in the same fashion, I have a specific system. I rank them in 3 different categories out of 5: Premise, Plot, and Execution.

  • Premise

    This would be the elevator pitch, the whole idea behind the anime.

  • Plot

    This is how the story is written, including characters, kind of how it'd be as a novel.

  • Execution

    This is everything else, from the visuals, and the moment to moment dynamic.

I would recommend anything on here that has a 5 in any category, regardless of the other ratings.

Tower of God drops us into a unique world that is totally unlike life as we know it, with the entire population's lives enamored by climbing the tower. And in this unique world, we also follow one of the greatest anomalies the tower has ever seen, leading to an intriguing and thoughtful premise. The plot is also quite good. We follow Baam who is chasing after a friend, while that sounds a little corny (looking at you Naruto), it makes sense considering Baam's original situation. The plot is strengthened by the many imaginative and interesting characters we meet along the way, along with the creative and thought-provoking stages throughout the tower.

Unfortunately, I think the anime adaptation was a bit lacking in the execution department. I felt that the animation was lackluster compared to the extragance of the tower, but I think it's hard to match up to the modern-day juggernauts like Demon Slayer. As a preface, I read most of the manhwa before watching the anime adaptation. With what's coming up, I hope the animation picks up and the studios get more budget to give the grandiose beauty that Tower of God will deserve.
